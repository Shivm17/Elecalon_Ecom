Cancellation & Refund Policy

Elecalon Ecom's guidelines for process withdrawal and payment return.

Slot Cancellation by Candidate

If a candidate decides not to proceed after slot confirmation, they must inform us within **24 hours** via email at support@elecalon.in.

Cancellations requested after the mentioned 24-hour period may not qualify for a refund.

Refund Eligibility

A **full refund** is applicable in the following cases:

  • The company cancels the process.
  • The candidate withdraws within **24 hours** of payment.

Refund Processing

Refunds will be credited within **7-10 working days** to the original payment method.

Non-Refundable Cases

A refund will **not** be provided in the following circumstances:

  • Failure to complete the joining process without valid reasons.
  • Providing false or misleading information during application.

For any refund queries, please contact us directly at support@elecalon.in.
